Compilation:
1. Compiler: GCC 4.7 or above (with std11 support)
2. Cmds:
Server: g++ -Wall -std=c++11 -g -pthread socket_utilities.cpp socket_server.cpp -o <server_bin>
Client: g++ -Wall -std=c++11 -g -pthread socket_utilities.cpp socket_client.cpp -o <client_bin>
3. Right now, a hard-coded user/passwd combination is used in server/client

Note:
std::thread is used instead of pthread library to save time
